About Me

I'm Josephine, a passionate full-stack programmer and tech copywriter. I'm a tech enthusiast who loves to code and write. I'm skilled in Python, Django, Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I hold a degree in Software Engineering, with a focus on backend development, from ALX. I also enjoy creating tech content for blogs and businesses. I'm dedicated to learning and staying up-to-date in this ever-evolving field.
